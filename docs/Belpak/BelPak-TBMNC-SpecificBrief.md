## Executive Summary

BelPak represents the **highest-priority opportunity** within the Red Arts Capital portfolio for Toyota Battery Manufacturing North Carolina supplier qualification. With 25+ strategically located facilities, proven OEM embedded operations experience (Subaru), and a 99.6% on-time-in-full delivery rate, BelPak is uniquely positioned to become Toyota's primary contract packaging and logistics partner for EV battery operations.

### The Opportunity
- **Estimated Annual Revenue:** $25-50M+
- **Service Categories:** Battery packaging, kitting, embedded logistics, hazmat handling
- **Strategic Fit:** BelPak's embedded operations model mirrors exactly what Toyota needs

### The Challenge
- **Certification Gap:** ISO 9001 and IATF 16949 certifications required
- **Hazmat Compliance:** DOT regulations for battery shipping
- **Systems Integration:** 29 facilities need unified ERP/MES platform

### The Solution
A focused Tier-1 supplier strategy with phased certification, systems integration, and embedded operations proposal.

## Part 4: Certification Requirements & Gap Analysis

### Required Certifications for BelPak

#### ISO 9001:2015 - Quality Management System
**Status:** GAP - Likely has systems but needs formal certification

**Requirements:**
- Documented quality management system
- Customer focus processes
- Risk-based thinking
- Performance evaluation
- Continuous improvement

**Timeline to Certification:** 3-6 months  
**Estimated Cost:** $15,000-30,000 per facility

#### IATF 16949:2016 - Automotive Quality Management System
**Status:** GAP - Critical requirement for automotive packaging

**Requirements:**
- All ISO 9001 requirements PLUS:
- Product safety requirements
- PPAP (Production Part Approval Process)
- APQP (Advanced Product Quality Planning)
- FMEA (Failure Mode and Effects Analysis)
- MSA (Measurement System Analysis)
- SPC (Statistical Process Control)

**Timeline to Certification:** 6-12 months  
**Estimated Cost:** $50,000-100,000 per facility

### Additional Compliance Requirements

#### Hazardous Materials Compliance
**Status:** GAP - Needs assessment

**Requirements:**
- DOT hazmat training for all handlers
- Proper packaging certifications
- Labeling compliance
- Shipping documentation
- Emergency response procedures

**Timeline:** 2-3 months  
**Estimated Cost:** $50,000-100,000 (company-wide)

**Requirements:**

- Safety program documentation

## Part 5: Addressing Key Business Concerns

### Concern 1: "BelPak is a service provider, not a manufacturer"

**From Dec 18 Meeting:** Critical insight that BelPak's certification needs differ from component makers.

**Response:**
- Service providers CAN and DO get IATF 16949 certified
- Certification scope focuses on packaging and logistics processes
- Subaru relationship proves automotive OEM acceptance
- Toyota specifically needs packaging/logistics partners

**Action:** Frame certification around packaging processes, not manufacturing processes.

### Concern 2: Customer Concentration Risk (30% in one customer)

**From Nov 14 Meeting:** Risk of over-reliance on single customer.

**Response:**
- Toyota adds diversification to stable automotive sector
- Automotive contracts are multi-year with renewals
- Less volatile than food/beverage innovation cycles
- Opens doors to other Japanese OEMs

**Action:** Position Toyota as strategic diversification, not concentration increase.

### Concern 3: Which facilities should be in scope?

**From Dec 18 Meeting:** Need to determine certification scope.

**Recommended Approach:**
1. **Phase 1:** Certify 2-3 facilities closest to NC
2. **Phase 2:** Expand to regional hubs
3. **Phase 3:** Full network certification

**Suggested Initial Facilities:**
- Atlanta, GA (Coregistics HQ - closest to NC)
- 1-2 additional Southeast facilities
- Total Phase 1: 3 facilities

### Concern 4: Systems Integration ("Too Many Versions of Truth")

**From Nov 24 Meeting:** Need unified ERP/MES across 29 locations.

**Response:**
- Brett Griffiths leading ERP/MES consolidation
- Single source of truth required for OEM compliance
- Toyota will require consistent reporting across facilities
- Investment enables scalability to other OEMs

**Action:** Include systems integration in Phase 1 scope.

### Concern 5: Culture Integration Post-Merger

**From Multiple Meetings:** Three family businesses need unified culture.

**Response:**
- Wellness Plan delivers immediate ROI ($640/employee/year tax savings)
- Insurance savings 3-4x tax savings
- Addresses Toyota's ESG requirements
- Creates unified employee experience

**Action:** Include Wellness Plan as "afterburner" in proposal.
