## Business Opportunity

**KDM Consortium Mission:**  
Build a vetted ecosystem of small/medium companies that can win more government and commercial contracts through opportunity identification, team assembly, proposal orchestration, and buyer access pathways.

**Target Market:**  
Small/medium businesses (10-200 employees) in logistics, cyber/zero trust, IT/data, training, engineering, energy resilience, and manufacturing services/support.

**Revenue Model:**
- Core Capture Member: **$1,750/month**
- Pursuit Packs (add-ons): Per-pursuit pricing
- Event ticketing, sponsorships, training courses
- **50/50 revenue split** between KDM and V+ on Net Program Revenue

**Revenue Projection (6 months):**
- 30 members × $1,750/month = **$52,500 MRR**
- **$315,000 annual run rate**
- **$157,500/year per party** (after 50/50 split)

## Technical Architecture

### Multi-Tenant Strategy
- **Single Codebase, Multi-Brand Deployment**
- Subdomain routing: `consortium.kdmassociates.com` or `kdm.strategicvalueplus.com`
- Brand configuration in database (logos, colors, messaging)
- Shared infrastructure with organization-based access control
- Potential for white-label offering to other consortiums

### Payment Architecture
```
Member Payment → Stripe Connect → Automatic Split:
  ├─ 50% to KDM (Merchant of Record)
  └─ 50% to V+ (Platform Provider)
```

### Security & Compliance
- ✅ HTTPS encryption (Vercel SSL)
- ✅ Firebase security rules
- ✅ Role-based access control
- ✅ PCI DSS compliance (via Stripe)
- ⚠️ SOC 2 Type II (future consideration)
